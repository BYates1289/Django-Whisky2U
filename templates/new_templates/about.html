OUR MISSION TO YOU

We Want To Fill As Many Mouths As Possible With The Greatest Whiskies From Around The The World.

Founded by a team of high-school friends way back in 2006 in Worcester, England, Whisky2U started off as a pipe dream. It wasn't until 10 years later, in 2016, that the team began to put their plans into action. Starting from very humble beginnings with not much knowledge about the retail industry, the team persevered, working night and day and have made Whisky2U one of the most popular UK services of it's kind. The love and friendship they share coupled with their passion for whisky and determination to provide their customers the best possible service is the perfect recipe for success.

We, at Whisky2U, understand that there is no such thing as a typical whisky drinker. That's why we have an adaptable approach to sales. We have customers who purchase a variety of monthly 5cl bottles, who wish to explore as many different drams as possible. We also have others who stick with their favourite tipple and purchase whenever they need replenishing. Anything you need, you can count on us!

There's no gatekeeping here, no tweed jackets or old school ties or any of that nonsense - just amazing whiskies heading straight to your door. Welcome to a better world of whisky.
